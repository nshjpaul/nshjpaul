TNT PROFILE
TNT COMUTER CONSULTANCY LTD is one of the leading computer firm that is specialize in Information Technology support and provide training service on Information Technology. It is an ICT firm, started in 2012 and officially registered in Rwanda Development Board on 4th December 2012 as Private Sector Company.

OUR VISION
﻿To become the leading IT hands on skills service provider in Rwanda and East Africa as a whole﻿
OUR MISSION
Provide computer support, promote and share IT skills,ICT INNOVATION
CORE VALUES
To reduce on ICT illiteracy levels
To create awareness about ICT products and services
To improve on the quality of life of people through hands on skills
OBJECTIVES
To assess the impact of ICT on students and Rwandans in general
To examine the challenges faced by Rwandans in ICT and provide solutions to them
To offer internship training services to students doing their research thereby making them completion on job market.
To improve on the quality of students by providing hands on skills in ICT hence gaining expertise and experience.
